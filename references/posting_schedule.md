## Business-Specific Adjustments

### Food & Beverage (coffee_shop, restaurant, bakery, food_truck)
- **Morning push:** 7-8 AM (catches commuters)
- **Lunch peak:** 11 AM-1 PM (decision time)
- **Evening cravings:** 5-7 PM (dinner planning)
- Avoid: Late night unless you're open late

### Fitness & Wellness (fitness_gym, yoga_studio, personal_trainer)
- **Early morning:** 5-7 AM (pre-workout crowd)
- **Lunch break:** 12-1 PM (midday motivation)
- **Evening:** 5-7 PM (after-work gym goers)
- Weekend mornings perform well

### Beauty & Personal Care (hair_salon, barbershop, nail_salon, dog_groomer)
- **Late morning:** 10 AM-12 PM (browsing/booking time)
- **Evening:** 7-9 PM (planning next appointment)
- Saturday mornings are peak engagement

### Retail (clothing_boutique, jewelry_store, pet_store, bookstore, florist)
- **Midday:** 12-2 PM (lunch break shopping)
- **Evening:** 7-9 PM (evening browsing)
- **Sunday:** High engagement for retail content
- New arrival posts: Tuesday or Wednesday

### Services (real_estate_agent, photographer, tattoo_shop, auto_detailing)
- **Morning:** 8-10 AM (planning/research time)
- **Lunch:** 12-1 PM
- **Evening:** 6-8 PM (portfolio browsing)
- Portfolio/result posts perform best mid-week

## Platform-Specific Notes

### TikTok
- Algorithm favors consistency over timing
- First 30 min after posting is critical for initial engagement
- Use trending sounds within first 48 hours of them blowing up
- Post at least 3x/week minimum to stay in the algorithm

### Instagram Reels
- Shares and saves weigh more than likes
- Cross-post TikTok content but remove watermark
- Use 3-5 hashtags (less is more since 2024 algorithm update)
- Stories can promote your Reel for extra reach

### YouTube Shorts
- Longer shelf life than TikTok/IG (evergreen discovery)
- Strong titles matter more here (search-driven)
- Can repurpose TikTok content directly
- Subscribe CTA in first comment works well
